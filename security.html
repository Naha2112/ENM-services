ENM'S Services - Security and Safety Solutions

Protecting your property, people, and peace of mind is our top priority. Our comprehensive security solutions are delivered by fully trained, SIA-licensed professionals who understand the unique challenges facing businesses and properties across the UK. From retail premises to construction sites, events to residential complexes, we provide tailored security services that adapt to your specific needs and budget.

SIA-Licensed Security Guards

Our highly trained security officers hold current SIA licenses and bring years of experience in threat assessment, conflict resolution, and emergency response. Whether you need visible deterrence, access control, or comprehensive site protection, our guards maintain professional standards while providing a welcoming presence for your legitimate visitors and customers.

CCTV Operators

Our certified CCTV operators provide round-the-clock monitoring services, ensuring your property remains under constant surveillance even when you're not there. Using state-of-the-art monitoring equipment and advanced camera systems, our operators can quickly identify potential threats, coordinate emergency responses, and maintain detailed incident logs for your records.

Door Supervisors

Creating safe, controlled environments for your venue is essential for both customer satisfaction and legal compliance. Our SIA-licensed door supervisors are trained in crowd management, ID verification, conflict de-escalation, and emergency procedures. They ensure your premises maintain appropriate standards while providing a professional first point of contact for your guests.

Event Security Staff

From intimate corporate gatherings to large-scale public events, our experienced security teams understand the complexities of event management. We work closely with event organizers to develop comprehensive security plans that address crowd control, VIP protection, emergency evacuation procedures, and coordination with local authorities when required.

Mobile Patrols

Our mobile patrol services provide flexible, cost-effective security coverage across multiple locations. Our marked patrol vehicles conduct regular site visits, perimeter checks, and alarm responses, offering visible deterrence while maintaining detailed patrol reports. This service is ideal for businesses requiring security presence without the cost of permanent guards.

Construction Site Security

Construction sites face unique security challenges including theft of materials and equipment, unauthorized access, and vandalism. Our construction security specialists understand these risks and provide targeted solutions including perimeter security, equipment protection, and coordination with site managers to ensure work continues safely and securely.

Concierge/Front of House Security

First impressions matter, and our concierge security staff combine professional hospitality skills with comprehensive security training. They manage visitor registration, access control, and building security while maintaining the welcoming atmosphere essential for modern commercial and residential properties.

Manned Guarding and Key Holding

Our manned guarding services provide continuous on-site security presence, while our secure key holding service ensures authorized personnel can respond quickly to alarms and emergencies. This combination offers comprehensive protection with the flexibility to scale services based on your changing security requirements.
