ENM'S Services - Property Maintenance Solutions

Maintaining your property to the highest standards shouldn't be a constant worry. At ENM'S Services, we provide comprehensive property maintenance solutions that keep your residential or commercial premises in excellent condition year-round. Our skilled tradespeople and project managers handle everything from routine maintenance to major renovations, ensuring your property retains its value and functionality.

Painting Services

Transform your property with our professional painting services. Whether you need interior refreshing, exterior weatherproofing, or complete color scheme changes, our experienced painters use premium materials and proven techniques to deliver flawless finishes. We handle surface preparation, protective coverings, and clean-up, ensuring minimal disruption to your daily operations.

Tiling Solutions

From bathroom renovations to commercial flooring projects, our tiling specialists work with ceramic, porcelain, natural stone, and modern composite materials. We provide expert installation, repairs, and maintenance for wall and floor tiling, ensuring waterproof seals and perfectly aligned finishes that stand the test of time.

Flooring Installation and Repair

Our flooring experts handle all types of surfaces including hardwood, laminate, vinyl, carpet, and commercial-grade materials. Whether you're updating a single room or reflooring an entire building, we provide professional installation, repairs, and maintenance services that enhance both appearance and functionality.

Electrical Services

All electrical work is carried out by fully qualified, certified electricians who ensure complete compliance with current regulations. From simple socket installations and lighting upgrades to complete rewiring projects and electrical safety inspections, we prioritize safety while delivering reliable, efficient electrical solutions.

Plumbing Services

Our certified plumbers handle everything from blocked drains and leaking taps to complete bathroom installations and central heating systems. We provide 24/7 emergency plumbing services alongside planned maintenance, using quality materials and modern techniques to ensure long-lasting, reliable results.

Renovations and Refurbishment

Whether you're updating a tired property or adapting spaces for new purposes, our renovation team manages projects from initial planning through to final completion. We coordinate all trades, handle necessary permits, and ensure projects are delivered on time and within budget while maintaining the highest quality standards.

Kitchen Refits

Transform your kitchen with our complete refit service. From initial design consultation through to final installation, we handle everything including cabinetry, worktops, appliances, plumbing, electrical work, and tiling. Our kitchen specialists create functional, attractive spaces that add value to your property.

Emergency Repairs and Call-Outs

Property emergencies don't wait for convenient times. Our emergency response team is available 24/7 to handle urgent repairs including burst pipes, electrical faults, security breaches, and weather damage. We respond quickly to minimize damage and restore your property to safe, functional condition.
