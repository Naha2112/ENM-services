Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, pricing, and process

General Questions

What areas do you serve?

We provide services throughout London and the surrounding areas. Our team can travel to most locations within the M25 and selected areas beyond. Contact us to confirm coverage for your specific location.

Are you insured and licensed?

Yes, we are fully insured with comprehensive public liability and professional indemnity insurance. Our security staff hold current SIA licenses, and all our tradespeople are qualified and certified in their respective fields.

How do I get a quote?

Getting a quote is easy! You can contact us via WhatsApp at +44 7356 238036 or email Admin@enmsservices.co.uk. We'll discuss your requirements and provide a detailed, no-obligation quote within 24 hours.

Property Maintenance

How long does a typical kitchen refit take?

A complete kitchen refit typically takes 2-4 weeks, depending on the size and complexity of the project. This includes removal of old units, plumbing and electrical work, installation of new units, worktops, and appliances, plus tiling and decorating.

Do you provide materials or do I need to supply them?

We can work either way. We can source and supply all materials as part of our service, or work with materials you've already purchased. Our quotes will clearly specify what's included, and we can advise on the best materials for your project and budget.

Do you offer emergency repair services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency repair services for urgent issues like burst pipes, electrical faults, or security breaches. Emergency callout charges may apply, but we'll always discuss costs upfront before proceeding with any work.

Security Services

Are all your security staff SIA licensed?

Yes, all our security personnel hold current SIA (Security Industry Authority) licenses appropriate to their role. This includes door supervisors, security guards, CCTV operators, and other security professionals. We can provide license verification upon request.

Can you provide security for one-off events?

Absolutely! We provide security services for all types of events, from small private parties to large corporate events and public gatherings. We'll work with you to assess your security needs and provide appropriate staffing and equipment.

How quickly can you deploy security personnel?

For emergency situations, we can often deploy security personnel within 2-4 hours. For planned assignments, we prefer at least 24-48 hours notice to ensure we can provide the most suitable personnel for your specific requirements.

Pricing & Payment

Do you charge for quotes and consultations?

No, all quotes and initial consultations are completely free with no obligation. We'll assess your requirements, discuss options, and provide a detailed written quote at no cost. You only pay if you decide to proceed with our services.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, cheques, and cash. For larger projects, we typically work on a staged payment basis with deposits and progress payments. All payment terms will be clearly outlined in your quote.

Do you offer guarantees on your work?

Yes, we stand behind all our work with comprehensive guarantees. The length and terms vary depending on the type of work performed, but we typically offer 12-month guarantees on workmanship and will honor manufacturer warranties on materials and equipment.
